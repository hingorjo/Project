Innovative Approach to Sustainable Aquaculture

Our research focuses on developing sustainable fish feed from wastewater-derived biomass, addressing both food security and environmental sustainability challenges in Qatar.

This approach not only reduces dependency on imported feed but also contributes to circular economy principles by valorizing waste streams.
